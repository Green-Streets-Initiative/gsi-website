## IN YOUR COMMUNITY

---

### Shift Ambassador *(replaces Walk/Ride Day Ambassador)*

**Tag:** Shift App

**Short description:**
Help people in your community discover the Shift app and log their first trip.

**Time commitment:**
🕐 2–4 hours/month

**Details:**
You go where the drivers are — school pickup lines, community events, farmers markets, neighborhood meetings — and start a different kind of conversation. Not "you should bike more," but "did you know you're probably already walking enough to earn rewards?" You help people download the app, log their first trip, and see what their short trips add up to. Success isn't talking to people who already bike. It's the person who had no idea their quarter-mile walk to the coffee shop counted.

Training included.

### Story Collector *(updated — phone, not tablet)*

**Tag:** What Moves Us

**Short description:**
Help commuters share their stories at community events and farmers markets during active campaigns.

**Time commitment:**
🕐 2–4 hours per event, occasional

**Details:**
You show up at a local event with your phone and a brief script. Inspire warm, short stories or audio snapshots about how people get around, how they got started, how it makes them feel. Then hand over the file — we do the rest.

### Rewards Partner Scout *(new)*

**Tag:** Shift App

**Short description:**
Help recruit local businesses to offer rewards for Shift app users in your neighborhood.

**Time commitment:**
🕐 2–3 hours/month

**Details:**
You know the coffee shop owner, the bike shop, the bookstore on the corner. We need people who can walk in, explain why offering a small discount to active commuters is good for their business, and make an introduction. You don't close the deal — you open the door. We'll give you a one-pager and a simple script. You bring the relationships.

### Neighborhood Lead *(new)*

**Tag:** Shift App

**Short description:**
Be the face of Shift in your neighborhood — drive adoption, organize challenges, and climb the leaderboard.

**Time commitment:**
🕐 3–5 hours/month

**Details:**
Every neighborhood on the Shift leaderboard needs someone behind it. You recruit neighbors to join, organize informal challenges ("Can we beat Porter Square this month?"), share results on local listservs and group chats, and keep the energy going. You're the reason your neighborhood shows up — and stays on the board.

## FOR SCHOOLS

### School Coordinator *(renamed from School Champion)*

**Tag:** School Program

**Short description:**
Be the point person who brings the Shift school program to a school you're connected to.

**Time commitment:**
🕐 Sufficient to get started: three one-hour check-ins

**Details:**
You're a parent, neighbor, or community member with a connection to a target school. You introduce the program to the right stakeholders, make the connection to GSI, help smooth the launch. You don't run the program — you open the door and help it get started.

Training included.
