Cashier Guide

How to process a Shift rewards redemption

1

Customer shows QR code

The customer will open the Shift app on their phone and show you a QR code on their screen.

2

Scan with your phone camera

Open the camera app on your phone (iPhone or Android) and point it at the QR code. A link will appear — tap it to open the verification page.

3

Tap "Confirm Redemption"

The web page will show the reward details and the customer's name. Tap the green Confirm Redemption button. Once confirmed, the reward is complete.

Can't scan the QR code?

Ask the customer to show the reward details screen in the app. You can verify the reward name, customer name, and timestamp visually. Each QR code can only be used once, so screenshots cannot be reused.
